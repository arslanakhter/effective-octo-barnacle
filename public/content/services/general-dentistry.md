# General Dentistry

Our general dentistry services form the foundation of optimal oral health. We provide comprehensive examinations, cleanings, and preventive care.

## Services Include:

- Routine checkups and cleanings
- Dental X-rays and diagnostics
- Cavity fillings and restorations
- Root canal therapy
- Tooth extractions
- Periodontal (gum) care
- Oral cancer screenings

Regular dental visits help prevent problems before they start, saving you time, money, and discomfort.
